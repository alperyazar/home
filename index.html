About me

Alper Yazar

“Full Stack Electronics” Engineer

I earned my BSc and MSc degrees from the same department, METU EEE, in 2012 and 2015, respectively. My master’s thesis focused on statistical signal processing. As a PhD candidate, I worked on FPGA-accelerated cloud computing.

At work, I mainly focus on FPGAs and embedded systems. I also have experience in analog/digital circuit design and PCB design.

I’ve been passionate about electronics and computers since I was 10. I enjoy working on projects, reading blogs, exploring code, watching technical videos, attending courses and webinars, publishing papers, and engaging in other geeky activities. I’m a big supporter of free and open-source projects 🐧.

Besides technology, I also love music and musical instruments 🎵.
